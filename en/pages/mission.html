The historical connotation of the CPC's original aspiration and mission

According to the report of the 19th CPC National Congress, the original aspiration and mission of the CPC is to seek happiness for the Chinese people and rejuvenation for the Chinese nation. Throughout the history of the CPC, whether in good times or bad, it has always pursued its original mission of "seeking happiness for the Chinese people and rejuvenation for the Chinese nation." In different historical periods, the party's original aspiration and mission have different historical connotations.

1. The Initial mission of the CPC in its early years

In February 1848, The Communist Manifesto drafted by Marx and Engels came out, which was a program for the communist League, the world's first proletarian political party, marking the birth of Marxism. Marx and Engels pointed out in the Communist Manifesto, "All movements in the past were of or for the benefit of minorities. The proletarian movement is the independent movement of the great majority, for the good of the great majority." "The Communists are not a special party in opposition to other workers' parties." "They have no interests distinct from those of the proletariat as a whole." "In place of the old bourgeois society, in which class and class antagonism existed, there is such a union, in which the free development of each is the condition for the free development of all." Marx and Engels declared to the world in the form of programs and declarations that it is the original aspiration and mission of the Communist Party to uphold the position of the people, work for the interests of the people and seek liberation for all mankind.

The salvos of the October Revolution brought Marxism-Leninism to China. When the Communist Party of China was founded in July 1921, Marxism-Leninism was written on its flag as the theoretical basis for the Party's guiding ideology. In the early days of the Communist Party of China, the party's original aspiration and mission set as its goal the overthrow of the oppression of the "three mountains" of imperialism, feudalism and bureaucrat-capitalism, the pursuit of national independence and liberation of the people, and the establishment of a better society in which no one exploits others.

2. The Party's original mission after the founding of New China

After the founding of new China, the first important thing was to restore the economy, and the CPC shifted its focus from the revolutionary war to economic construction. In 1953, MAO Zedong accurately stated the Party's general line for the transitional period as follows: "This is a transitional period from the founding of the People's Republic of China to the basic completion of socialist transformation. The general line and task of the Party in this transitional period is to basically realize the industrialization of the country and the socialist transformation of agriculture, handicrafts and capitalist industry and commerce within a fairly long period of time." By the end of 1956, China had basically completed the socialist transformation of ownership of the means of production, realized the transformation from a new democratic society to a socialist society, and established the socialist system. In March 1958, comrade MAO zedong put forward "going all out, aim high, multiple construction economically socialism" of the general line, and in May 1958, eight second meeting of the communist party of China formally approved the route is based on the party's main task, based on the accurate judgment, reflects people's urgent requirement changes the backwardness of economy and culture in our country's common wish. Between 1949 and 1978, the Communist Party of China led and united the people of all ethnic groups in China in initially meeting the basic needs of the Chinese, who account for one quarter of the world's population, such as food and clothing.

After the founding of new China, the Original aspiration and mission of the Party was to fully restore the economy, establish the basic socialist system, and fundamentally resolve the situation that the New China was in a state of complete ruin. Represented by comrade MAO zedong the communist party of China led the people across the country for the country prosperous, people's happiness and make unremitting struggle, established the basic system of socialism, has established the independent and relatively complete industrial system and national economic system, the comprehensive construction socialism, and historic great progress, people's material and cultural life level gradually improve. The party's original theoretical achievements and great achievements in socialist revolution and development have provided valuable experience, theoretical preparation and material foundation for starting socialism with Chinese characteristics in the new historical period.

3.The Party's original mission in the reform and opening up period

The construction and development of socialism in China is an unprecedented great cause. Although great achievements have been made, setbacks have also been encountered and detours have been taken. In December 1977, Deng Xiaoping met with The Chairman of the Communist Party of Australia, Mrs. Hill and His wife, and said, "What is superiority? Do not work, do not study called superiority? Is the people's living standard backward rather than improved called superiority? If this is called the superiority of socialism, we can not have such socialism." During an inspection tour of the three northeastern provinces in September 1978, Deng Xiaoping said, "We need to think about how much we have done for the people." "We are too poor, too backward and frankly sorry to the people." In December 1978, Deng Xiaoping made a speech at the closing meeting of the Central Work Conference, saying, "If we do not carry out reform now, our cause of modernization and socialism will be buried." The CPC Central Committee with Comrade Deng Xiaoping at its core has pursued reform and opening up in all respects, proceeding from reality, the basic national conditions in the primary stage of socialism, and the fundamental interests of the Chinese people and nation. Reform and opening up have greatly stimulated the creativity of the people, unleashed and developed the productive forces, and boosted the vitality of social development. The people's living standards have significantly improved, China's overall national strength has increased and its international standing has risen significantly. China has gone through the modernization process that some countries have gone through in more than 100 years or even longer. From 1978 to 2012, China made remarkable achievements in its socialist modernization drive. China's economy grew at an average annual rate of 9.8%, much higher than the 6.1% annual rate between 1953 and 1978 before the reform and opening up and more than three times the average annual rate of the world economy during the same period.

During the period of reform and opening up, the original aspiration and mission of the CPC was to remove all ideological and institutional obstacles impeding the development of the country and the nation, and to unite and lead the people in carrying out the great new revolution of reform and opening up. Comrade deng xiaoping as the main representative of the unity of the communist party of China led the whole party and the Chinese people of all ethnic groups to emancipate the mind, seeking truth from facts, and by the third plenary session, completely abandoned the error of "taking class struggle as key link", the party and the country focus shifted to economic construction, has made the major decision of China's reform and opening-up, determines the basic line in the new period, This enabled China to realize a historical transition from a highly centralized planned economy to a dynamic socialist market economy, and from a closed and semi-closed economy to all-round opening-up. We thoroughly revealed the nature of socialism, determined the basic line for the primary stage of socialism, made it clear that we should go our own way and build socialism with Chinese characteristics, scientifically answered a series of basic questions and development strategies for building socialism with Chinese characteristics, and succeeded in creating socialism with Chinese characteristics.
