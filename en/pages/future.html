Beijing 2022 Winter Olympics and Paralympics

The XXIV Olympic Winter Games, also known as The 2022 Beijing Winter Olympic Games, are scheduled to open on Friday, February 4, 2022, and close on Sunday, February 20. On September 17, 2021, the theme slogan of the Beijing Winter Olympic and Paralympic Games -- "Together toward the future" was released. On October 18, the flame of the Beijing Winter Olympic Games was lit in Greece. On October 20, the flame of the Beijing Winter Olympic Games arrived in Beijing.

To ensure the success of the Beijing Olympic Winter Games and Paralympic Winter Games is a major event of the Party and the country, and it is our solemn commitment to the international community.

-- Remarks by Xi Jinping, general secretary of China (CPC) Central Committee, during an inspection tour in Beijing and Hebei province and during a briefing on Beijing's preparations for the 2022 Winter Olympics and Paralympics from January 18 to 20, 2021

On July 31, 2015, Beijing and Zhangjiakou won the bid to host the 2022 Winter Olympics and Paralympics together. Over the past five years, the Beijing Olympics and winter paralympic games preparations for synchronous propulsion, phased achievements have been made Beijing Olympic venues and infrastructure, orderly, event organization notification service security work comprehensive advancement, promotion is on the rise, deep to conduct international exchanges and cooperation, sustainable development and heritage of achievements in their work.

Venue construction is a top priority for hosting the Beijing Winter Olympics and Paralympics. We must adhere to the Olympic standards, reverse the construction schedule, build and renovate stadiums and major supporting infrastructure in an orderly manner, and ensure that they are completed and put into use on time with good quality. We should highlight the characteristics of science and technology, wisdom, green and frugality, pay attention to the use of advanced scientific and technological means, strictly implement the requirements for energy conservation and environmental protection, protect the ecological environment, cultural relics and historic sites, and display Chinese style

-- Xi Jinping, general secretary of the COMMUNIST Party of China central Committee, President and Chairman of the Central Military Commission, at a visit to grassroots officials and people in Beijing, February 1, 2019

Beijing, Yanqing and Zhangjiakou, All 12 competition venues were completed, Steady progress was made in the construction of non-competition venues. The winter Olympic Villages in the three competition areas are basically completed. Implementing the principle of giving priority to ecological protection throughout the process; Maximize use of existing venues and facilities, Building or retrofitting venues and facilities to green building standards, Protect the ecological environment of the competition area and promote the harmony between man and nature.

Beijing Region: The ice Ribbon, the national Speed Skating stadium, is the only new ice competition venue, It is the world's largest ice surface cooled by transcritical direct evaporation of carbon dioxide.

Yanqing District: Beijing Winter Olympic Games construction the most difficult competition area. With the core design concept of "Mountain Pavilion and Ecological Winter Olympics", Focus on building "pavilions hidden in mountains", Adhere to "avoid" as the first principle, A total of 11027 rare plants were transplanted near the ground.

Zhangjiakou Region: Genting Ski Park is a venue for freestyle skiing 100,000 square meters of underground storage tanks. "Light up the lamp of Beijing with the wind of Zhangbei" : For the first time in Olympic history, all venues will be covered with green electricity, Zhangbei Flexible DC project connects the scenic power in Zhangbei area to Beijing power grid.

With the theme of "The Next Decade", Invited Chinese first Yang liwei into space, table tennis world champion deng yaping, famous director Stanley tong, youth singer and actress Jackson, Mars toastmasters founder Martin, professor of international relations at store Yin, youth actor shen month, taobao anchor eu emilia, mountaineering hero Xia Boyu, former Chinese women's volleyball team players, women Song Xi HuiReQi, Peking University, who director of men's wear brand jun-hong wang , Dongfeng Motor engineer Bian Ning and other 13 representative guests from the fields of technology, sports, film, media and other fields shared their wonderful life stories and looked forward to the next decade through the form of speech.

Yang Liwei, China's first man in space, spoke about the selection process of China's first astronauts. After five years and six months of hard training, he became the first astronaut to fly the Shenzhou V manned space project with the highest overall ranking. He revealed that to become an astronaut to go through extremely cruel "devil training", that is a human physiology, psychology and will of the ultimate challenge, not ordinary people can bear. Recalling how he felt when he went into space in 2003, Yang said, "I flew alone in space, but I knew I was not fighting alone. At this moment, I represent all Chinese people. What I saw proved the success of China's space technology. In the next decade, China's space industry will enter a magnificent new era, and our goal is the sea of stars."

Mountaineering hero Xia Boyu tells of his indisicable connection with Mount Qomolangma. 1975 was a "year of change" for Xia boyu: he lost his father, his legs, and Everest became a distant dream. But life never knocked him down. Forty-three years later, at the age of 69, Xia finally stood on the summit of Mount Qomolangma, more than 8,800 meters high, achieving his "moment of glory". Speaking on the Power Up stage, he said: "Everest, I want to thank it. It is the dream of climbing Mount Qomolangma that has supported and inspired me so far that makes my life richer, more meaningful and worth remembering." Xia said his next dream is "7+2" -- the highest peaks on the seven continents and the North and South Poles.

Olympic champion Deng Yaping created a memory of The Times that belongs to every Chinese. On stage in the Next Decade, she shared the hardships behind the glory. Because of her short stature, Deng Yaping was not favored in the provincial team and the national team. Facing criticism, she told herself at that time, "In terms of sports performance, I am no worse than anyone else. Why can't I get into the provincial team when everyone else can? I had to prove I was the stuff." As for the future development of sports, she said, "No matter what time the older generation left behind, the perseverance and hard work should never be lost; We should never forget the fighting spirit of the older generation."
