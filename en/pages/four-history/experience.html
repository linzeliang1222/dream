Valuable experience: A summary of the Century-old history of the CPC

The Communist Party of China has accumulated valuable experience in its century-old history, which is precious and can be summarized as follows.

1. We must uphold Party leadership over all work. China's modern history shows that without the COMMUNIST Party there would be no New China, and without the Communist Party there would be no socialism with Chinese characteristics. General Secretary Xi Jinping has pointed out that "the Party leads everything in the party, government, military and people's studies, east, west, north and South." The most essential feature of socialism with Chinese characteristics is the leadership of the COMMUNIST Party of China. The greatest strength of the system of socialism with Chinese characteristics is the leadership of the Communist Party of China, and the Party is the highest force for political leadership. We must resolutely uphold the authority and centralized, unified leadership of the CPC Central Committee with Comrade Xi Jinping at its core, improve systems and mechanisms for upholding overall Party leadership, and enhance the Party's ability and resolve to set the direction, plan for the overall situation, formulate policies, and promote reform.

2. We must uphold the guiding position of Marxism. The Communist Party of China is a proletarian political party established under the guidance of Marxism. Marxism is the ideological and spiritual banner of the Party. General Secretary Xi Jinping has pointed out that "Marxism is the fundamental guiding ideology for building the Party and the country. If we deviate from or abandon Marxism, our Party will lose its soul and its direction." We must integrate the basic tenets of Marxism with China's reality and the characteristics of The Times, constantly adapt Marxism to Chinese conditions, arm the whole Party with the Party's innovative theories, educate the people, and constantly open up new horizons for Marxism in contemporary China and in the 21st century.

3. We must adhere to the path of socialism with Chinese characteristics. Direction determines our future, and our path determines our destiny. As General Secretary Xi Jinping has pointed out, "Socialism with Chinese characteristics did not fall from the sky. It is a fundamental achievement made by the Party and the people through untold hardships and enormous costs. Socialism with Chinese characteristics is not only a great cause we must continue to advance, but also a fundamental guarantee for our future." We must unswervingly stay on the path of socialism with Chinese characteristics, and neither follow the old path of isolation and ossification, nor follow the evil path of abandoning the banner and becoming an independent school.

4. We must continue to put the people first. The Communist Party of China has no interests of its own, still less special interests, except those of the working class and the overwhelming majority of the people. As Xi Jinping has pointed out, "The people are the biggest source of confidence for our Party to govern, the solid foundation of our Republic, and the foundation for us to strengthen the Party and rejuvenate the country." Our Party comes from the people, works for the people, and thrives on them. We must always have one heart and soul with the people, share weal and woe with them, and work with them in unity. We must adhere to the fundamental purpose of serving the people wholeheartedly, implement the mass line, respect the principal position of the people and their initiative, and always maintain close ties with the people. We must build a mighty force that inspires unity, and unite and lead the people in jointly creating historic undertakings.

5. The Party must continue to give top priority to development in governing and rejuvenating the country. Poverty is not socialism. It is the essential requirement of socialism to liberate and develop the productive forces and enhance the comprehensive national strength of a socialist country. As Xi Jinping has pointed out, "Development is the foundation and the key to solving all problems in China." We must adhere to the new vision of development, take promoting high-quality development as the theme, build a modernized economy, and lay a strong material foundation for socialism with Chinese characteristics.

6.We must continue to deepen reform and opening up. Reform and opening-up is the source of vitality for contemporary China's development and progress. It is an important weapon for the Party and the people to make great strides to keep up with the pace of The Times, and the only way to uphold and develop socialism with Chinese characteristics. As Xi Jinping has pointed out, "Reform and opening-up is a great new revolution carried out by the people under the leadership of our Party in the new era. It is the most distinctive feature of contemporary China and the most distinctive banner of our Party." Reform and opening up is only in progress, but never finished. We need to deepen our understanding of and application of the laws governing reform and opening-up, strengthen our resolve, strengthen our courage, and unswervingly carry it through to the end.

7. We must uphold democratic centralism. Democratic centralism is the fundamental organizational principle of Marxist political parties. Our Party has enriched and developed the content of democratic centralism in its long-term practice, making it a unique leading and working system of the Party. As Xi Jinping has pointed out, "Democratic centralism is the fundamental organizational principle and leadership system of our Party, and an important mark that distinguishes A Marxist party from other political parties." Under the new circumstances, we must uphold this fundamental organizational principle and maintain and give full play to the Party's greatest institutional advantage.

8. We must adhere to scientific, democratic and law-based governance. Scientific, democratic and law-based governance is a governance mode suited to China's national conditions that has been explored and formed by the CPC in its long-term practice of governance. General Secretary Xi Jinping pointed out: "Must adapt to the national modernization process, improve the level of scientific ruling, democratic ruling, ruling in accordance with the law, improving national institutions started their ability, improve the people to manage state affairs according to law, economic social and cultural affairs, the ability of their own affairs, party, state and social affairs management institutionalized, standardization, sequencing, We will continue to improve our ability to effectively govern the country with the socialist system with Chinese characteristics." We must continue to develop and improve the Party's scientific and effective form of governance.

9. We must uphold socialist ideology. The guiding position of Marxism in the field of ideology is the ideological foundation for the concerted struggle of the whole Party and the Chinese people. General Secretary Xi Jinping pointed out: "To do publicity and ideological work well under the new situation, we must consciously assume the mission of raising the flag, gathering the people's support, educating new people, revitalizing culture and developing our image." We must firmly cultural self-confidence, adhere to the socialist core values guide cultural construction, vigorously carry forward the national spirit with patriotism as the core and with reform and innovation as the core spirit of The Times to strengthen the construction of socialist spiritual civilization, for all-round construction of socialism modernization country provide a powerful ideological guarantee and strong cultural support.

10. We must uphold the Party's absolute leadership over the people's armed forces. Having a strong army and consolidating national defense are the basic conditions for the proletarian party to seize power and stay in power for a long time. General Secretary Xi Jinping has pointed out that "building a people's army that listens to the Party's command, can fight and win, and maintains a fine style of conduct is strategic support for realizing the two Centenary Goals and realizing the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation." Our army was conceived and developed under the leadership of the Party. Upholding the Party's absolute leadership over the armed forces is the eternal soul of the armed forces and the fundamental guarantee for the Party's long-term governance and the country's long-term peace and stability. We will unswervingly follow the path of strengthening the armed forces with Chinese characteristics, strengthen them through political work, reform, science and technology, talent, and law-based governance, comprehensively modernize national defense and the armed forces, and build the people's armed forces into world-class forces.

11. We must continue to consolidate and develop the broadest possible patriotic united front. The united front is an important magic weapon of the CPC. To establish a united front, achieve the broadest political solidarity, expand the social base and unite all forces that can be united is a successful experience for the CPC to achieve victory in various historical periods. As General Secretary Xi Jinping has pointed out, "The united front has never been built for the sake of looking good or sounding good, but because it is useful, useful and indispensable. In the final analysis, the united front is the work of being a human being, and the united front is to build up the strength to work together." We should continue to develop and strengthen the patriotic united front for the new era, promote harmonious relations between political parties, ethnic groups, religions, social strata, and compatriots at home and abroad, seek the greatest common ground, draw the greatest circle of concentric circles, and add strong strength to the reunification of the motherland and the realization of the Chinese dream of national rejuvenation.

12.We must continue to advance the lofty cause of peace and development for mankind. The COMMUNIST Party of China (CPC) is a party that strives for the happiness of the Chinese people and the progress of mankind. The CPC has always taken it as its mission to make new and greater contributions to mankind. General Secretary Xi Jinping has pointed out, "All the CPC does is to seek happiness for the Chinese people, rejuvenation for the Chinese nation, and peace and development for mankind." We will raise the banner of peace, development and cooperation, win-win, adhere to grasp the world development trend, always stand in the tide of development, unswervingly pursue the road of peaceful development, unswervingly pursue the win-win strategy of opening up, adhere to promote the building new international relationships, promote to build human destiny community, strengthen the friendly exchanges with other countries, and people from all around the We will continue to advance the lofty cause of peace and development for mankind.

13. We must comprehensively govern the Party with strict discipline. The greatness of the COMMUNIST Party of China does not lie in never making mistakes, but in never avoiding medical problems, facing up to problems and having the courage to make self-revolution. General Secretary Xi Jinping pointed out: "To forge iron, one must be strong. The key to running China's affairs lies in the Party, and the key lies in the party's efforts to supervise and comprehensively govern it with strict discipline." The fundamental reason why the CPC has come to the fore amid repeated contests among various political forces in modern China is that it has always maintained the spirit of self-revolution, taken up the scalpel again and again to correct its own ills and solved its own problems on its own. If our Party is to remain qualified to lead a great social revolution, it must have the courage to carry out a great self-revolution, constantly enhance its capacity for self-purification, self-improvement, self-innovation, and self-improvement, and always maintain its advanced nature and purity. We need to improve our ability to manage the Party, advance the great new project of Party building, and make the Party even stronger.

The Communist Party of China is determined to achieve great things in the future. The centenary is in its prime. Looking back at history, we are proud; Looking ahead, our hearts swell. History moves from yesterday to today and then to tomorrow, and the ties between history cannot be cut off. In the century since its founding, the COMMUNIST Party of China has united and led the Chinese people to create historical glory. The achievements of the CPC today have provided the premise, created the conditions and laid the foundation for greater achievements tomorrow. Staying true to its original aspiration, keeping its mission firmly in mind, and working hard all the time, the CPC will surely write new chapters and create new and greater achievements in the centenary of its governance, that is, the centenary of the founding of the People's Republic of China. History has created the CPC through the people's exploration and struggle, and the CPC has united and led the people to create historical glory. "If you look at history, you will see the future." To review the century-old history of the CPC, we should strengthen the CPC's confidence in its history, and at the same time, strengthen the confidence of the Chinese people and the Chinese nation in the future.

Serve the people heart and soul
