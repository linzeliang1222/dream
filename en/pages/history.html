Create the world

  • The birth of the Chinese communist Party

    In July 1921, the communist Party of China was founded, which is the historical starting point of the communist Party of China's one hundred years of endeavor, but also the historical starting point of the vicissitudes of Chinese society. The great logic of historical development determines the historical inevitability of the birth of the CPC.

  • The workers' and peasants' movement emerged

    In August 1921, the Secretary department of the China Labor Union was established in Shanghai to lead "the communist Party's legal and open labor movement". Starting with the Hong Kong seamen's strike in January 1922 and ending with the Beijing-Han railway workers' strike in February 1923, the communist Party of China organized workers and other masses and set off the first climax of The Chinese workers' movement.

  • KMT - CPC cooperation set off a revolutionary wave

    Further understanding of China's national conditions, the Party determined the anti-imperialist and anti-feudal democratic revolutionary program, and according to the comintern's instructions, determined the policy of establishing a united front. The third National Congress of the communist Party of China, held in Guangzhou in June 1923, decided that members of the communist Party should join the Kuomintang as individuals to achieve cooperation between the Kuomintang and the communist Party.

  • The agrarian revolutionary war was magnificent

    After the failure of the Great Revolution, the Nanchang Uprising led by the Party was the first shot of armed resistance against the Kuomintang reactionaries. The 1987 Conference decided on the general policy of agrarian revolution and armed uprising. The Party then launched the Autumn Harvest Uprising, the Guangzhou Uprising and armed uprisings in many other areas. The autumn harvest uprising in the Hunan-Jiangxi border area led by MAO Zedong established the first rural revolutionary base in Jinggangshan. In July 1937, the Lugouqiao Incident broke out and the War of Resistance against Japanese aggression broke out.

  • The War of Resistance against Japan saved the nation from danger

    In 1937, the whole nation's War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression broke out. The party promoted the second cooperation with the Kuomintang and established the anti-japanese national united front. The sons and daughters of The Chinese people are united as one, united as one, all parties, all nations, all classes, all classes, all groups of common enemy, to fight together.

  • The strategic decisive battle of the People's Liberation War

    After the victory of the War of Resistance against Japanese Aggression, the Party put forward the policy of peace, democracy and unity and tried to establish a democratic coalition government. But the Kuomintang tore up the armistice agreement and the CPPCC agreement, launched a large-scale attack to the liberated areas, launched a full-scale civil war. The Central Committee of the communist Party of China issued the party instruction "Crush Chiang Kai-shek's attack by self-defense war", and then decided to take "overthrow Chiang Kai-shek" as the final policy to solve the domestic problems.

  • The founding of the People's Republic of China

    On October 1, 1949, 30, 000 soldiers and civilians gathered in Tian 'anmen Square, held the founding ceremony. The founding of the People's Republic of China was one of the great events that shook the world in the 20th century. It marked the end of China's new democratic revolution and the beginning of a new historical era for the Chinese nation. The history of the Party also opened a new chapter.

  • During the New-democratic Revolution, the communist Party of China made its first great historical contribution. It united and led the Chinese people in a 28-year bloody struggle to defeat Japanese imperialism, overthrow the reactionary rule of the Kuomintang, complete the New-democratic Revolution and establish the People's Republic of China. The meaning of this great historical contributions is that ended completely semi-colonial and semi-feudal old China social history, thoroughly to end the state of the old China fragmented and abolish the powers unequal treaty imposed on China and all the privileges of imperialism in China.

Change the world

  • Establish the CPC leadership system

    With the birth of new China, the communist Party of China (CPC) changed from a party that led the people in their struggle to seize national political power to a party that led the people in seizing national political power for a long time.

  • Establish a basic socialist system

    From 1949 to 1956, when socialist transformation was basically completed, the CPC established a series of basic socialist systems under the leadership of the communist Party of China, laying the fundamental political premise and institutional foundation for contemporary China's development and progress.

  • The first five-year plan

    To realize industrialization and modernization and change China from a poor and backward country is the goal that the Chinese communists are striving hard for. After the founding of New China, we began to formulate and implement the first five-year Plan (1953-1957) for developing the national economy.

  • Major projects will be carried out

    In the 1950s and 1960s, a series of important engineering projects were built in China in the process of building socialism in an all-round way. The Chengdu-Chongqing Railway, built in 1952, was the first independently built railway in New China. The Wuhan Yangtze River Bridge, completed in 1957, was China's first railway and highway bridge over the Yangtze River. In 1959, the ten largest buildings in Beijing, which took one year to build, were completed and put into use.

  • To science and technology

    In the 1950s, the CPC Central Committee and The State Council put forward the slogan of "marching toward science and technology". In 1956, the Outline of the Long-term Plan for The Development of Science and Technology 1956-1967 was drawn up. By 1962, the Plan had been completed ahead of schedule. In 1963, the second long-term plan for the development of science and technology in China was formulated, the 1963-1972 Plan for the Development of Science and Technology. China's emerging industries such as radio, semiconductors, electronic computers, atomic energy and aerospace have developed rapidly.

  • The Second great historical contribution of the CPC is that it united and led the Chinese people in completing the socialist revolution, established the basic socialist system, eliminated all systems of exploitation and advanced socialist construction.

Earthshaking

  • Achieve a great historical turning point

    After the "Gang of Four" was crushed, the Party and the state pushed forward the process of putting things right. In December 1978, the Third Plenary Session of the 11th CPC Central Committee was held in Beijing. This marked the beginning of the historic transformation of the Party and the state from "class struggle as the platform" to economic development as the central task, from rigidity and semi-rigidity to comprehensive reform, and from seclusion and semi-seclusion to opening up. It marks the beginning of a new era of reform and opening up and socialist modernization.

  • The tide of reform and opening up is rising

    The reform in the new era will start from the countryside. In 1982, the household contract responsibility system became the main form of agricultural production responsibility system. After the 12th CPC National Congress, the economic system reform focusing on cities began to unfold.

  • Open doors that have been closed and semi-closed for a long time

    Along with the reform, the policy of opening to the outside world was carried out. To a certain extent, opening up is also reform. The main forms of opening to the outside world include attracting and utilizing foreign investment, introducing technology, foreign trade and establishing special economic zones.

  • Socialist market economy

    The economic restructuring initiated at the Third Plenary Session of the 11th CPC Central Committee is actually a process of constantly introducing, giving play to, strengthening and expanding the role of the market mechanism. Party and the country to establish and perfect the socialist market economy as the main line, promote, state-owned enterprises, rural finance, taxation, finance, foreign trade, foreign exchange, investment, price.

  • The "three-step" strategy and the construction of a well-off society

    In April 1987, Deng Xiaoping clearly put forward the strategic concept of "three steps". The 13th National Congress of the communist Party of China (CPC) officially set a three-step development strategy for China's modernization drive. The goal of the second step of the "three steps" strategy is to enter a well-off society. By the end of the 20th century, the first two goals of the "three-step" strategy had been successfully achieved, and the people had on the whole achieved a moderately prosperous life. This is a great victory for the socialist system and a new milestone in the development history of the Chinese nation.

  • Establish socialism with Chinese characteristics

    In His opening speech to the 12th CPC National Congress in September 1982, Deng Xiaoping pointed out, "We have drawn the basic conclusion by summing up our long-term historical experience to combine the universal truths of Marxism with the concrete realities of our country, follow our own path and build socialism with Chinese characteristics."

  • China's national strength has made great strides

    The reform and opening up policy has made great strides in China's national strength. From the ranking of China's GDP in the world: in 1980, it was 301.5 billion DOLLARS, ranking eighth in the world. After 30 years of development, by 2010, it was $5,878.6 billion, overtaking Japan to become the world's second largest economy. This change clearly illustrates the vitality, achievements and contributions brought about by reform and opening-up.

  • Since the third plenary session of the eleventh, made the third great historical contribution of the communist party of China, is the unity led the Chinese people are great new revolution of reform and opening-up, stimulated the people's creativity, the liberation and development of social productivity, greatly enhance the vitality of social development, people's living standards improved significantly, and the comprehensive national strength enhanced markedly, significantly improve the international status.
