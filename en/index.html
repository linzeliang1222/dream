"Four histories" -- The history of the party, the history of new China, the history of reform and opening up, and the development history of socialism

Xi jinping pointed out that general secretary "only adhere to the ideological construction, the theory of strong party, don't forget the beginner's mind to more consciously and more firmly bear the mission to".

The history of the party is that the party of China started from scratch

Guided by Marxist thought, the communist party of China has constantly drawn on historical experience in practice to advance its political system building in line with the changes of The Times and become a mature political party with strong political leadership. In its history of development, the CPC has also encountered crises.
The new Chinese history is China under the leadership of the communist party.

Since the founding of New China, the communist party of China has made full use of the Marxist theory of state, and gradually established and consolidated the state system, political system, fundamental system, basic system and important system of New China. In view of China's actual conditions.
The history of reform and opening up is a socialist system

Reform and opening-up, initiated at the Third Plenary Session of the eleventh Central Committee of the communist party of China (CPC), is a policy of both internal reform and external opening-up. In essence, it aims to benefit the people and rejuvenate the nation in the new era. The communist party of China led the people in overcoming.
The development history of socialism fantasy to science.

Since the founding of the People's Republic of China, China has actively explored the road of socialist construction. Forty years of reform and opening-up, socialism is no longer a foreign theory knowledge,Deng Xiaoping Theory and the theoretical system of socialism with Chinese characteristics,built the great cause of socialism with Chinese characteristics, and created new glory for world socialism.

The Rise Of China

The communist party of China is determined to achieve great things in the future. The centenary is in its prime. Looking back at history, we are proud; Looking ahead, our hearts swell.History moves from yesterday to today and then to tomorrow, and the ties between history cannot be cut off.Young people should shoulder the historic mission, forge ahead with confidence, be ambitious, be virtuous, be capable, and undertake major responsibilities.
